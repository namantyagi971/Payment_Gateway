About Us

Welcome to the Naman Tyagi Foundation

Naman's ambitions for a better India have grown beyond the educational arena to creating a better tomorrow for the underprivileged youth of our country. He is committed to build youth focused programs, which connect children to resources and use his partnerships to setup support services to the vulnerable section of our society.

We believe in research, and our team is constantly meeting with innovators and leading experts to understand the problems faced by our disadvantaged youth and how we can make the most of our resources to drive real action which will leave a lasting impact and build a better future for our India.
